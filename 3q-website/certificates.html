Certifications

3Q launched an exclusive Advanced Professional Certification platform first time in India for fresh graduates in the field of Infrastructure Management services. The graduates who feel they have acquired enough skills can directly proceed to 3Q Certification exams and get themselves as Certified professionals which is a game changer in attracting high demand/paid corporate jobs by adding the 3Q official badges on their CVs. Those who wish to gain knowledge can undergo training/internships offered at 3Q or 3Q authorized training partners across the country.
